Hi, I’m Nick. I’m in the process of creating something of an email list. I’m trying to make it different than your typical email-distro or newsletter.

I want to make something that allows me to chat openly with those around me and inspires one-on-one discussion between myself and whomever cares.

The emails I send won’t come with a regular cadence (as far as I know, keep in mind, I’m still feeling this out). In it, will undoubtedly be my travel schedule, some things I’m thinking about, some gifs, and usually a longer piece: the purpose of the email.

I’m going to try as hard as possible to make this more than just another email in your inbox, I want this to be a way for us to communicate.

If this sounds like something interesting to you, please check it out, signup below, and let me know what you think.
